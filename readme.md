### Emphasis[^3]
***

> ``** __``     bold text       `**bold** __bold too__`
> ``* _``       italic text     `*italic* _italic too_`
> ``~~``        strikethrough   `~~strikethrough~~`
> ``combine!``  combine em!     `**_italicbold_** ~~_italicstrikethrough_~~`
> ``~ ~``       subscript       `CO~2~`
> ``^ ^``       superscript     `e=mc^2^`
> ``==``        highlight       `==highlight==!`
